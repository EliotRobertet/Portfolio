About me

My name is Eliot Robertet, and I am 21 years old. I am currently completing my final semester of a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Iceland. I am a hardworking and driven individual with a strong passion for engineering sciences.

Outside of my academic studies, I have a wide range of interests, including weightlifting, tennis, golf, and skiing. I have played tennis for many years, representing the Icelandic men’s national team, and also worked as a tennis coach for five years. These activities reflect my passion for staying active and embracing both individual challenges and team dynamics.
